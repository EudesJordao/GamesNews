COD Black Ops 6 on Game Pass: Microsoft confirms in new trailer that the game will arrive at launch on the subscription service

Xbox confirmed news that many fans were waiting for, Call of Duty: Black Ops 6 will arrive directly in the Game Pass catalog at launch. COD 2024 has not yet had much information revealed, but a trailer released this Tuesday (28) confirmed that the game will reach the subscription service.

In the trailer, we can see some political figures debating and talking about the truth and the world, nothing is very clear, and the theme that "the truth lies" returns in this preview. Then, the video cuts to the soldier on the game's cover and the video ends. We still don't know much about the plot and gameplay of COD Black Ops 6, but news should be presented during the Xbox Showcase on June 9th.
