MultiVersus has a peak of more than 100 thousand players on Steam after relaunch

A few days after being relaunched, last Tuesday (28), MultiVersus once again had a peak of more than 100 thousand simultaneous players on Steam, the main platform for selling games on PC. According to data collected by SteamCharts, which measures the popularity of games on the platform, MultiVersus had 114 thousand simultaneous players in the last 30 days.

The numbers are similar to those at the launch of the MultiVersus beta, in July 2022, when the game had its record of 153 thousand simultaneous players. The average number of players at launch was 66,000, but there were huge drops, 37%, 79% and 67% in the following three months, and the number never returned to where it was at the start of the beta until the servers closed in June. 2023.
