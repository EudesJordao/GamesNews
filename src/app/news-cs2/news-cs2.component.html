CS2 Majors without open qualifiers in Europe and the Americas

Valve's new update to the Majors regulations has put an end to open qualifiers in Europe and the Americas! Organizer of the next Major, Perfect World revealed this Friday the dates on which the main tournament will be played (November 30th to December 15th) and the RMRs, in-person qualifiers that will all be played in Shanghai. The Major's host city will receive its own RMRs which, among the new updates to the regulations, will no longer have direct invitations to teams that advanced to the 2nd stage of previous Majors, another of the big news.

Increasingly interested in having its ranking active and a reference for everything on the circuit – especially with the end of partner leagues in 2025 – Valve will use its Regional Standings to award invitations to RMR and closed qualifiers.
